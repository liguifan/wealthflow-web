Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Wealthflow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Wealthflow does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not collect financial information or payment data.
  • We do not use analytics tools that identify individual users.

2. Account Registration

The App requires you to create an account to access certain features. Account credentials (such as your email and password) are used solely for authentication purposes and are not used for marketing, advertising, or sold to third parties. We store only the minimum information necessary to provide the service.

3. Data Sources

All investment data displayed in the App is sourced from publicly available government disclosures, including filings made under the STOCK Act. This data is public information and is not linked to any individual user of our App.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for authentication or backend infrastructure. These services are bound by their own privacy policies and are selected for their commitment to data security and minimal data collection.

5. Children's Privacy

Wealthflow is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Disclaimer

Wealthflow is an informational tool only. The data displayed in the App is sourced from public government filings and is provided for informational purposes only. Nothing in the App constitutes investment advice. You should always conduct your own research and consult a qualified financial advisor before making any investment decisions.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
